About HYPAGS

HYPAGS GUI (Version 1.4.0)

Aaron Peche
Federal Institute for Geosciences and Natural Resources, BGR

HYPAGS (HYdraulic Parameters And Grain Sizes) model enables to retrieve virtual sieve curves (by means of representative grain sizes d₁₀, d₂₀, d₅₀, d₆₀ [m]) and hydraulically relevant parameters effective porosity nₑ [-] and hydraulic conductivity K [m/s] by single input values of K, d₁₀, d₂₀.

⚠️ PLEASE NOTE: The model is valid for coefficients of uniformity Cᵤ <~ 3.

⚠️ PLEASE ALSO NOTE: For a more flexible use of HYPAGS, check out the pedon package by Martin Vonk: https://github.com/martinvonk/pedon

Cite as:
